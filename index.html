What is its role: to bring in Traffic or to drive Spend?

Higher scores from shoppers on one or more of the "Traffic" measures indicate an ability to impact the trip before the store, whereas higher scores on "Spend" measures show impact once in the store.

Is it high or low engagement in this retailer?

Shoppers tell us whether they intended this to be a high speed or more considered purchase (Grab and Go/Browse). And then if something changed this in store (either positively or negatively in their view).

Where does it sit in the Intentionality/Engagement Matrix?
